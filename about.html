MY STORY

I'm Raid Akrich, a web designer and developer based in Spain. With years of hands-on experience crafting everything from marketing sites to interactive apps, I've honed my eye for clean aesthetics and functional code. What gets me going is seeing a design leap off the screen and actually work- that's the thrill I'm always chasing.

My background comes from years of self-directed learning and building sites purely out of curiosity. What started as tweaking templates turned into fully custom projects, each one teaching me something new about design and code. Along the way, I've experimented with different tools and techniques, gradually shaping a style that's clean, functional, and focused on the user.

Looking ahead, I'm keen to team up with businesses that share a straightforward, no-nonsense approach. My goal's simple: take those creative skills I've built up and help put solid products in the spotlight.
